Telemetry & Data Usage

Please review how optional analytics works

We respect your privacy and give you full control over telemetry.

PrivateAI runs entirely on your device. We never transmit your chats or files. If you choose to help us improve, we will collect a small, anonymous set of telemetry events. Reviewing the full disclosure below ensures you know exactly what is (and is not) shared.

What is collected (only if you opt-in)

  • Aggregated feature usage counts (e.g., how often document chat is opened)
  • Performance metrics such as app launch time, inference latency, and model load time
  • Crash reports that include anonymized stack traces and error codes
  • Operating system version, hardware class (CPU/GPU family), and available RAM
  • License tier so we can prioritize support (no personal identifiers included)

What is NEVER collected

  • Your prompts, responses, or any conversation content
  • Documents, filenames, or snippets of your files
  • Keyboard input, screen recordings, or browsing history
  • Emails, IP addresses, or other direct identifiers
  • Telemetry while you are offline (events are queued locally and you can discard them)

How telemetry is handled

  • Events are batched, encrypted, and sent only when an encrypted connection is available.
  • You can clear pending telemetry or disable collection at any time from Settings â†’ Privacy.
  • Data is retained for 30 days for trend analysis, then permanently deleted.
  • We never sell telemetry data and never use it for advertising or profiling.

This telemetry is entirely optional. Declining has no impact on feature access. Opting in helps us prioritize bug fixes, performance improvements, and new capabilities that benefit everyone.

Your choice: Please select whether you want to share telemetry data to help improve PrivateAI.
